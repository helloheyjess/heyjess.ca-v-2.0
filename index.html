Get to know me!

Every day, I work hard to bring compelling content-driven designs to life with clean, meaningful, accessible markup—and I LOVE it! I specialize in writing modular, maintainable, user-focused code resulting in websites that are as close to future-proof as possible. Never one to turn away from a challenge, I am happiest when I am operating at the edge of my abilities and I put a lot of care into leveling up my developer chops.

I currently spend my days at HackerYou and I am looking for a full-time front-end web development position beginning at the end of August, 2015. I also enjoy creating fun and functional web demos with HTML5, CSS3 and jQuery. Check me out on CodePen to see what I'm up to!

When I'm not coding, you might find me participating in my other passions: running or riding my bike around Toronto, hanging out with my dog, or working on a DIY project.
