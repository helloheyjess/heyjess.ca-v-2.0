Get to know me...

I am a web designer and developer based in Toronto, ON and my modus operandi is create, collaborate, and solve problems.

For as long as I can remember, I have had a passion for creating. After enjoying a decade as a freelance musician, and 5 years as a marketing & communications professional, I found myself returning to an old love of mine: web development.

I currently spend my days coding at HackerYou. When I'm not coding, you can find me running or riding my bike around Toronto, hanging out with my dog, or getting really into a DIY project.
