Get to know me!

Every day, I work hard to bring compelling content-driven designs to life with clean, meaningful, accessible markup—and I LOVE it! I also get a kick out of creating fun and functional web demos with CSS and jQuery. Check me out on CodePen to see what I'm up to!

Never one to turn away from a challenge, I am happiest when I am operating at the edge of my abilities and I put a lot of care into leveling up my developer chops. Ultimately, my goal is to write easy-to-maintain code that is as close to future-proof as possible.

I currently spend my days at HackerYou. When I'm not coding, you can find me running or riding my bike around Toronto, hanging out with my dog, or working on a DIY project.
